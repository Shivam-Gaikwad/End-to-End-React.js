we can create an function inside the tag also
can create outside the tag and simply call inside the tag


eg:

function displayOnclick(){
    console.log('button wa clicked');
};

<button Onclick ={displayOnclick}> Click me </button>

or

<button Onclick ={function displayOnclick(){
    console.log('button wa clicked');
}}> Click me </button>

or arrow function 

<Button Onclick={ ()=>{
    console.log('Button was clicked ')
}}> Click me </Button>
